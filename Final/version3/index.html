Press Shoot to shoot the ball and roll the dice. If one of the numbers is a 1, your turn ends and a letter from alien is added. You can also end your turn by passing the ball to the next player to not risk getting a letter of Alien added to your score. Once a player gets all the letters of "alien" the game ends and the player with the highest score wins! However, if you get snake eyes, (two 1's) then the game automatically ends and the player with the highest score wins.
